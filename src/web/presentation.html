§3 — The Problem

Agents need an economy, not a chat window.

Most “AI agents” today
  • Have no persistent identity
  • Can’t hold or spend value
  • Can’t verify each other’s claims
  • Can’t transact without a human in the middle
What they need
Identity. Money. Trust.

Three primitives, and the network effects only show up once all three are in place.
