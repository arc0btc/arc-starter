Empirical parameter calibration over theoretical defaults: When system gates use thresholds (x402 volume cutoffs, min transaction sizes), tune based on actual operational volumes rather than industry standards or theoretical maximums. Conservative parameters may be 50x too high for agent-scale operations — calibrate downward empirically and re-baseline after major operational changes.
